About TechWave Semiconductor

Pioneering the future of semiconductor technology

Our Story

Founded in 2010, TechWave Semiconductor began with a simple mission: to create innovative semiconductor solutions that power the technologies of tomorrow. What started as a small team of engineers with big ideas has grown into a global leader in semiconductor design and manufacturing.

Our journey has been defined by technological breakthroughs, strategic partnerships, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. Today, TechWave Semiconductor products can be found in millions of devices worldwide, from smartphones and automobiles to industrial equipment and IoT devices.

As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what's possible in semiconductor technology, enabling our customers to create products that change the world.

Our Mission

To develop semiconductor solutions that enable our customers to create innovative products that improve people's lives while minimizing environmental impact.

Our Vision

To be the world's most trusted partner for semiconductor solutions, known for our innovation, quality, and commitment to sustainability.

Our Values

  • Innovation: We constantly push the boundaries of what's possible
  • Quality: We are committed to excellence in everything we do
  • Integrity: We act with honesty and transparency
  • Sustainability: We design with the future in mind
  • Collaboration: We achieve more by working together
